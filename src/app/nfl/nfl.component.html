NFL Pick 2 Challenge

Here are the rules....

  • You have to pick 2 games every week.
  • You may not choose the same team twice.
  • Selections can only be made before kickoff of that game.
  • Once you choose a team to win, you may no longer select that team in future matchups.
  • Scores will automatically update every Tuesday afternoon.
  • You will be sent an email if you have not made your selections about 4 hours before first kickoff.
  • You will be sent an email on Tuesday evening of your updated scores, available picks, and leaderboards.
  • The following shows the scoring breakdown
    • - 50pts - Your pick wins.
    • - 5pts - For each Touchdown your winning team scores (Only if your team wins).
    • - 3pts - For each Field Goal your winning team scores (Only if your team wins).
    • - 2pts - For each Safety your winning team is awared (Only if your team wins).
    • - 0pts - Your pick loses.
