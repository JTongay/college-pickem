NCAA Top 25 Pick Challenge

Here are the rules....

  • All games available are matchups that involve a College team in the top 25.
  • There will always be an underdog.
  • You must pick who will win each matchup.
  • Your personal score will automatically update every Monday at noon.
  • An Email will be sent to you every Monday giving you an update on your score, and your ranking compared to other players.
  • Another Email will get sent to you every Wednesday to remind you to make your selections if you haven't.
  • The following shows the scoring breakdown:
    • - 1pt - You choose the correct winner and the team was favored
    • - 3pt - You choose the correct winner and the team was an underdog
    • - 0pt - You choose incorrectly
